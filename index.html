    Presentation

    This application is accesible for everyone who want to improve his skills in running. Who want to have a good time in the next marathon? With this application (Android and web site), it's possible to follow exactlty the progression of the training with graphs, survey, ...

    This auto-adaptive system can bring you very usefull information about your training (temperature, cardiac frequence, time) and make smart analysis with all the data.
